About Our Store

Premium motorcycle parts. Trusted by riders. Built for performance.

BUILT FOR RIDERS

Kickbarks Moto Shop was created for riders who share the same passion for motorcycles, performance, and the riding lifestyle. What started as a simple idea to provide quality motorcycle parts has grown into a store dedicated to supporting the riding community.

We understand what riders truly need because we are riders ourselves. Every product we offer is carefully selected to help maintain, customize, and enhance motorcycles for both daily use and long rides.

Our shop is built to provide reliable parts, trusted upgrades, and essential accessories that riders can depend on. We aim to make every purchase convenient, dependable, and worthwhile for the riding community.

OUR PURPOSE

Our purpose is to support the motorcycle community by making high-quality parts and accessories accessible to every rider. We believe that every rider deserves dependable products, fair pricing, and a smooth shopping experience.

Whether you're upgrading performance, improving safety, or maintaining your motorcycle, we strive to be a trusted partner in your riding journey and ensure every rider can shop with confidence and convenience.

COMMUNITY FIRST

Kickbarks Moto Shop is more than just an online store — it is a space built for motorcycle enthusiasts who value quality, performance, and trust.

We aim to build strong relationships with our customers and fellow riders by providing honest service, reliable products, and continuous improvements to our platform. Our focus is not only on selling products but also on supporting the community that keeps the riding culture alive.

WHAT WE OFFER

We offer a growing selection of motorcycle parts and accessories carefully chosen for quality, durability, and performance. Each product is selected to ensure reliability and value for money.

From maintenance essentials to performance upgrades and riding accessories, our goal is to provide products that enhance your riding experience and keep your motorcycle performing at its best.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to become one of the most trusted motorcycle parts and accessories stores within the riding community. We aim to build a brand known for quality products, dependable service, and genuine support for riders.

As we grow, we remain committed to improving our store, expanding our offerings, and providing a better shopping experience for every rider.

OUR COMMITMENT

  • Providing quality and reliable motorcycle parts
  • Delivering a smooth and secure shopping experience
  • Supporting riders and the motorcycle community
  • Continuously improving our products and services

We are not just a shop — we are built for riders, by riders.
